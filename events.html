Journal Club

Wednesdays 1pm, NHB 4.202. To get notifications, or to sign up to present, e-mail Jayanth R T at jayanth.r.t@utexas.edu.

This journal club is a forum for discussing neurons, brains, and behavior among people with diverse backgrounds. Graduate students and postdocs are especially encouraged to attend and propose/present papers. Appropriate papers present neural data, theoretical models, principles for computation, new statistical, mathematical, or machine learning methods etc.


You can also suggest papers, presenters etc. by adding comments on this sheet.
