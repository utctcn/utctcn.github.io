Research tools and software

Brainlife.io
Website
Free cloud platform for secure neuroscience data analysis.
Easy Amp Control
Website
Easy Amp Control is developed with C++ and FLTK on a Mac to control a BVC-700 amplifier through ITC-18.
Ephic (Electrophysiology and Imaging with C++)
Website
With Ephic, you can simultaneously control a fast, low noise, and quantitative CCD camera through an EDT DV C-Link frame grabber board, and an amplifier through a HEKA ITC-18 data acquisition interface. The system is good for voltage imaging, calcium imaging, whole cell recording, and field potential recroding. Ephic is equipped with intuitive and versatile tools to control the experiment, and contains several powerful tools to analyze both imaging and electrical data.
EPM Analyzer
Website
EPM Analyzer is developed to evaluate video clips of elevated plus maze test (EPM), which is a test to evaluate the anxiety level of rodents. EPM Analyzer counts the number of closed arm entries, the number of open arm entries, and time in the open arms. It also records the coordinates of the body center and the resident arm at each time point.
Eyelid Tracker
Website · Paper
Quantify eyelid position during eyelid conditioning experiments.
FST Analyzer
Website
This program is developed for the analysis of modified Forced Swim Test (FST) videos of animals.
Generalized chi-square distribution
Website · Paper
Matlab toolbox to compute the statistics, pdf, cdf, inverse cdf and random numbers of the generalized chi-square distribution.
Image processing with natural scene statistics
Website
We measure the statistical properties of large sets of natural images. These are then used to perform digital image processing tasks such as enlargement (super-resolution), denoising, and realistic camera noise synthesis.
Integrate and classify normal distributions
Website · Paper
Matlab toolbox to integrate normal (Gaussian) distributions in any dimensions with any parameters within any domain, compute pdf/cdf/inverse cdf of any function of a normal vector, and compute quantities concerning classification performance among two or more multinormals, such as error matrix and discriminability d'.
neuroGLM
Website · Paper
Using generalized linear models to fit and analyze trial-based spike responses of multiple neurons with complex response properties.
OFT Analyzer
Website
This program is developed for the analysis of Open Field Test (OFT) videos of animals.
PLDAPS (PLexon DAtapixx PSychtoolbox)
Website · Paper
A hardware architecture and Matlab software toolbox for neurophysiology that requires complex visual stimuli and online behavioral control.
Neuroscience lab protocols
Website
Protocols published by the Harris lab on tissue preparation, microtomy, electron microscopy, 3D reconstruction and lab safety.
Reconstruct
Website
Trace and annotate slice images, and combine to create a 3D reconstruction. Reconstruct has been used to make 3D images of brain cells, blood vessels, the brain of an ant, human fetus bone, a sea lily, a fish fossil, metal alloys, tree bark, and spider genitalia.
WGO (Waveform Generator and Oscilloscope)
Website
WGO is developed to test ITC-18, a computer interface to control other instruments, such as amplifiers, picospritzers, shutters, CCD cameras, etc. Programming support for ITC-18 is provided for IGOR and C/C++. One of the best ways to test the characteristics of a computer interface like ITC-18 is to simulate one waveform generator and one oscilloscope. WGO is developed with C++ and FLTK and can run on either a Mac or a PC.
Zero-Inflated Gamma probabilistic model
Website · Paper
This code implements the Zero-Inflated Gamma encoding and decoding model for post-deconvolved calcium imaging traces.
